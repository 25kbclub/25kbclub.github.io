25KB Club

The modern internet is bloated more and more to full bloat!

The 25KB club came to add and create a club where alternative websites have their space. We went back to the initial culture of the internet where people didn't follow standards but made their own art ;).

25KB Inspired by the clubs:

  • 250kb.club
  • 512kb.club
  • 1mb.club

> Rules for becoming a member

  1. The website not exceed 25 KB compressed.

  2. Website need must have a reasonable amount of information.

  3. Advertising sites in general are not allowed.

  4. 25kb club dicided if website is accepted or not.

> Submit your website

Only send email [25KB Club] submitted URL" as the subject line. In the body of the email, add the size of the page.
There are several ways to check the size, we recommend using: https://tools.pingdom.com/

The process may take a few days... (We need work to eat.)
